Hide When Out Of Stock

 [TEXT : 1]

Select this check box to hide the product automatically when you run out of inventory. Adding stock back to the Stock Status field automatically un-hides it.

If this product was created as the child of a parent product through the Inventory Control Grid, we recommend against selecting this check box. See Smart Match to learn more about automatically hiding out-of-stock child products.

This product will only be automatically hidden if the quantity drops below 1 due to a purchase. Manually setting the stock to 0 (or a negative amount) will not hide the product. Hidden products are still accessible through direct link or URL entry.

+ expand: Exact Fieldname in Database: Hide_When_OutOfStock
