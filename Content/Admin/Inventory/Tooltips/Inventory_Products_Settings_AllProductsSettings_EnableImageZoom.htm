Enable Image Zoom

Select this check box to turn on a magnification feature for all product images. When visitors hover over the image or any selected alternative view photos, they can view an extreme closeup of the portion selected.

Note that for this feature to work, a product's larger product photo must be at least 300 pixels wide or 300 pixels high, and at least 20% larger than the medium-sized product photo.
