Warehouse ID(s)

 [TEXT : 255]

The options you created at Inventory > Warehouses display in the Available list. Click one of your choice and click the right arrow icon (>) to move it to the Selected menu if this product ships from a different location than the one you established at Settings > Shipping > Ship From Location. Doing so ensures that live rate shipping calculations are accurate.

For more information, see Warehouses.
