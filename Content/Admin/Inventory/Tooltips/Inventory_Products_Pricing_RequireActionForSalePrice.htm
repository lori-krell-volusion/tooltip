Require Action for Sale Price

 [TEXT : 10]

Make a selection from this drop-down menu if you want to hide the sale price and require shoppers to take a specific action before they can receive it:

Add to Cart: When you select this option, an Add to cart for lower price link displays on the product details page below the normal price. When shoppers click it or the Add to Cart button, the product is added to the cart at the sale price.

Email Better Price: When you select this option, an Email me a better price link displays on the product details page below the normal price. When shoppers click it, a dialog box opens explaining that you are unable to display the sale price and inviting them to submit their email addresses. When they do, they automatically receive an email disclosing the sale price with a link to the product details page, where they can complete the purchase at the sale price.

+ expand: Exact Fieldname in Database: HowToGetSalePrice
