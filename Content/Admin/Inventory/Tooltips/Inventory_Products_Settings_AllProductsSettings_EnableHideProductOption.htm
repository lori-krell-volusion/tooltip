Enable Hide Product Option

  

Select this check box to display a Hide Product check box on each product edit page at Advanced Info > Product Display. Selecting the Hide Product check box hides the product from the storefront search and any categories it belongs to. Visitors can still access the product and add it to the cart if they enter the exact URL or follow a link to the exact URL, including through search engines.
