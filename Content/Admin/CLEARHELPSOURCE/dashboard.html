Common Questions

Can I customize my dashboard?

The dashboard is intended to give you a quick overview of your store's most important figures and trends each time you log in. You can customize the time period to view order totals over the last week, month, or 3 months, and show top products and categories over the last day, week, or month.

For in-depth and highly customizable reports, go to Reports > Reporting.

How do I manage my Volusion account?

To access and manage your Volusion account, click My Volusion under the Resources header in the right navigation menu of your Dashboard. Log in using the email address and password you set up for your initial trial.

For information on what you can do with your myVolusion page, please see the Accessing the myVolusion page.

Documentation

The Volusion Dashboard gives a visual snapshot of how your store's performing right now, including:

  • Orders - select week, month, or 3 months to view order totals over the specified period. Click View Orders to go to the Orders page
  • Top 5 Products - select week, month, or 3 months to view the top selling products over the specified period.
  • Top 5 Categories - select week, month, or 3 months to view the categories with highest sales over the specified period.
  • Trends - this shows trends in orders, sales, and average sales per customer. Click the View Reports link to open the Generate Reports page.

The rest of the page gives quick links to support site resources, the most recent entries in the Volusion Blog, and a handy To Do list where you can set up a list of outstanding action items.
