Mobile Commerce

Use Volusion's mobile commerce features to help your store serve the fast-growing retail segment of mobile shoppers.

Common Questions

How do I enable my store's mobile experience?

  1. Under the Mobile Storefront heading, set the switch to On.
  2. Under the Mobile Checkout heading, set the switch to On.

Having both settings turned On will allow shoppers on mobile devices to have an optimized browsing and checkout experience.

How add a logo to my mobile store?

  1. Go to Design > Logos.
  2. Click Browse next to Mobile Storefront Logo and fine your logo image file on your computer. Once you've made a selection, your file name will display next to the Browse button. Note that the logo should be saved as a .jpg file type, and the recommended size is 320 x 80 pixels.
  3. Click Upload.

The logo will display at the top of your customers' screen throughout the browsing and checkout experience.
