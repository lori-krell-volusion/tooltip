Common Questions

How do I get started?

You can get started by connecting your eBay seller account with your Volusion store.

  1. Select the country/currency of the regional eBay site where your seller account is set up from the Select an eBay Site dropdown.
  2. Click Go to eBay to open the Grant application status page, where you should read eBay's terms and click I agree.
  3. On the Grant Access step, you'll see the status of your access. Once you see the Access Granted! message, your eBay seller account and your Volusion store are connected.
  4. Click Get Started to start creating your first product listing using Volusion's eBay integration.

How do I create an eBay listing from my Volusion store?

You can create listings directly from your Volusion store products, making it easy to list your products on eBay.

  1. Click Add on your Ebay Listings page.
  2. Select the product your want to list and choose the Primary eBay Category.
  3. Enter the required product, payment and shipping information.
  4. Click Review Listing to review your listing before submitting it to eBay.
  5. Click Okay, List on eBay to agree to the eBay fees and submit your listing to eBay.

Based on the Primary eBay Category you select, you will have different available fields and values in the description, price, payment and shipping sections. Required fields are indicated with an asterisk ( * ), but we recommend providing as much information as possible for your product listing.

How do I list products with multiple options?

You'll need to create separate listings for each product option or option combination. For example, if you sell t-shirts in 3 different colors, you need to create a separate eBay listing for each t-shirt color.

Once you've created your first listing for a product with options, use the Duplicate feature to quickly recreate the product and modify the description as needed.

Note that products with required options must have unique child product codes or they are not eligible to be listed through the Volusion eBay Integration feature.

How do I duplicate a listing?

Creating a duplicate of a product listing can save you a lot of time if you're listing similar products or products with multiple options.

  1. Find the product you want to duplicate in your eBay Listings table.
  2. Click Duplicate in the far-right column.
  3. Edit your product details as needed to create a new unique listing.

Getting Started

First, you have to connect your Volusion store with your eBay seller account. Do this by following these easy instructions:

  1. Select the country/currency of your eBay seller account from the Select an eBay Site dropdown.
  2. Click Go to eBay to go to eBay's Grant application status page. Here, read eBay's terms and click I agree.

That's it! Your eBay seller account is now connected to your Volusion store and you can begin creating product listings and processing eBay orders within Volusion.

Creating Product Listings

Creating product listings is easy with Volusion's eBay integration!

  1. Select an eligible product from your Volusion store to get started.
  2. Enter a unique Listing Title and enter the Quantity of this product that you'd like to list on eBay.
  3. Select a Primary eBay Category. This selection will determine the available fields and selections in the Description, Price, Payment and Shipping sections of the Create eBay Listing page.
  4. Enter the description fields. All required fields are marked with a red asterisk.
  5. Enter the price and listing details in the Fixed Price Settings section.
  6. Click Edit Payment Methods to add accepted payment methods to your product listing. Note that all payment methods must be configured in your eBay seller account to function with this product listing.
  7. Select the Shipping Methods you will use to ship the product to your eBay customer. Enter the Item Location information to display on the product listing.
  8. Click Review Listing to view the details you've entered, agree to the eBay listing fees and list the product on eBay.

Your product listing is complete! Now, when a customer purchases a product from this Volusion-generated product listing, the order will be available to process at your store's Orders > Process Orders table!

eBay Orders in Volusion

When someone purchases a product through an eBay listing, you'll receive the order in your Volusion store for processing and shipping. You can view eBay orders in your Admin Area under Orders > Process Orders.

All of your eBay orders will contain the following information:

  • In the Order Total box at the top of the page, you'll see that This Order Was Placed Via eBay.
  • All features of the Payment Log box will be disabled.
  • The customer email will include an ebay_ prefix.

Since the payment for each eBay order will be processed through your eBay seller account, all Payment Update features will be disabled. Additionally, since the order is completed in eBay, you will not be able to make updates to the contents or price of the order.

Click Complete Order to send the Order Shipped email to the customer and enable additional features such as the Order Review Email. This will send the shipping order notification to your eBay seller account so the customer can check on the status of the order within eBay. You can also print shipping labels and packing slips from this page to assist you in completing and shipping the order.
