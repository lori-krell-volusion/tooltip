Offensive Language Filter

Documentation

This page allows you to specify words or terms that you deem offensive to filter incoming content from customer product reviews.

How the Filter Works:

When a review is submitted to your Volusion store by a registered customer, the review's title and content are compared to all words or phrases programmed into the Offensive Language Filter. If any words or phrases within the filter match words or phrases within the customer review, it will then be placed into the list of offensive reviews within your Volusion store, where only store administrators can view it.

This provides you the chance to examine each filtered review and decide if you wish to edit or outright delete the review.

Adding and Removing from the Filter:

The Offensive Language Filter comes pre-configured with some common, offensive terms.

You can add a new word or term to your content filter by clicking the Add button near the top of the filter table, entering the word or phrase you wish to add to the filter and then clicking Save.

You can delete any entry within your content filter by marking the check box to the right of any term and clicking Save.

Note that when adding new content to the Offensive Language Filter you must enter all terms using upper-case characters. Items programmed into the Offensive Language Filter are not case sensitive. Any matches found within the content of a review will be filtered regardless of case.
