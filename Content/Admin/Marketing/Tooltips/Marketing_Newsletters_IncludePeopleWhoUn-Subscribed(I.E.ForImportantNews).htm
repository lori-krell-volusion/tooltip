Include people who un-subscribed (i.e. for important news)

 [TEXT : 1]

Select this check box to send the newsletter to all email addresses listed in your customer database, regardless of whether or not they are newsletter subscribers.

Note: We strongly recommend against using this option, as sending messages to unsubscribed users in many cases constitutes a violation of your Terms of Service and the CAN-SPAM Act. It should only be used for urgent messages that you know non-subscribed customers would want to receive, such as product recalls.
