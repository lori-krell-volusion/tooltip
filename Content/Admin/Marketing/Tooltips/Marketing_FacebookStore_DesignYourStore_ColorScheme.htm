Color Scheme

Choose from a selection of preset color themes for your Facebook store, or create your own. To choose, click the Preset Themes tab or the Customize tab.

Preset Themes:

Use the left and right arrows to scroll through preset choices. To apply one, click it and save.

Customize:

There are five color elements you can customize: Background Color, Text, Link Color, Accent Color, and Accent Text Color. You can use the color picker to the right of each element, or type a hex code directly in the text field. The Preview image on the left shows the theme as you create it.
