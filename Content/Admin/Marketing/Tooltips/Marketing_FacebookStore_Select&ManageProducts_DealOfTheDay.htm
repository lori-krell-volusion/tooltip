Deal of the Day

  

This value controls how the Deal of the Day displays on your Facebook store when both are turned on:

No, do not display: Hides Deal of the Day. This is the default option.

Display the Featured Deal only: Displays the top deal on the Deal of the Day page, below the logo and custom text / HTML section (if any) and above the products sections (if enabled).

Display all active deals: Displays all active deals from your Deal of the Day page.

+ expand: Exact Fieldname in Database: FieldName
