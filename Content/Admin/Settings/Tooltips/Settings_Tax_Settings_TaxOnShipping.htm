Tax on Shipping

  

Select how you would like taxes to be charged on shipping costs.

Do not tax: Tax will not be charged for shipping costs.

Tax when taxable items exist: Tax will be charged for shipping costs only when tax is charged on the order items.

Always tax: Tax will be charged for all shipping costs, regardless of whether any products have been marked as taxable.
