Enable Social Store

  

Once you have added the Volusion Social Store application to your business page in Facebook, you can use this setting to open or close the store without having to change any settings within Facebook. Closing your store will cause the Store Closed Message to display. Please be aware that selecting this check box is not the only step to opening a Facebook store. Please see The Volusion Facebook Store for complete instructions.
