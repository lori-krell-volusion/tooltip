Enable Order Editing

  

When you select this check box, registered customers can log in to their accounts and edit the address details, product quantities, and shipping methods on their orders, provided that no administrator has already completed or locked the order. Note that orders are automatically locked if your gateway is set to either Authorize at Sale, Capture at Shipping (default and recommended) or Authorize and Capture at Sale. PayPal orders are also automatically locked.
