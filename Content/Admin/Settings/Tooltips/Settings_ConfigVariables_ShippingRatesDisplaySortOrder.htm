Shipping Rates Display Sort Order

  

The value in this field determines the display sequence of your shipping rates in the shipping rates drop-down menu at checkout.

If you enter "Manual" in this field (default value), methods display according to the Display Order On Checkout field on each method edit page (in the Advanced Settings section).

If you enter "PriceAscending" in this field, methods display by price, with the least expensive first.
